English terms for balms, basils, betony, coleuses, deadnettles, ground ivy, hyssops, lavenders, mints, oreganos, patchouli, perilla, sages, savories, selfheal, skullcaps, thymes, and other plants in the family Lamiaceae, also known as the Labiatae.
